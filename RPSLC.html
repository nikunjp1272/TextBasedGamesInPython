How to Play?

This is an interesting game from The Big Bang Theory (series) where all you have to do is select one of the five
options given below. Choose one of the 5 options from the table given below to proceed. After you choose your
character, you'll know who wins from the rules mentioned below.

Rules:

  1. Rock Beats Paper and Lizard
  2. Scissors Beats Lizard and Paper
  3. Spock Beats Scissors and Rock
  4. Lizard Beats Spock and Paper
  5. Paper Beats Spock and Rock
The rules mentioned are strictly based on the win conditions of each of the option.
